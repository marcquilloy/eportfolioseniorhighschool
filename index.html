About The School

Laguna State Polytechnic University Los Baños Campus Senior High School (LSPU-LB SHS) is dedicated to delivering quality education that equips students with the skills and knowledge necessary for success in higher education and the workforce. As part of LSPU’s esteemed academic system, our senior high school department offers specialized tracks, including Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM); information and Communications Technology (ICT); accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM); humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS); and Technical Vocational Livelihood (TVL). With experienced educators, state-of-the-art facilities, and a dynamic learning environment, LSPU-LB SHS promotes innovation, critical thinking, and leadership among students. We take pride in shaping future professionals and industry leaders, ensuring they are well-prepared to face the challenges of the modern world. Join us at LSPU-Los Baños Senior High School, where excellence meets innovation!

Vision,Mission,Quality Policy

Vision
LSPU is a center of technology innovation that promotes interdisciplinary learning, sustainable utilization of resources, collaboration and partnership with the community and stakeholders
Mission
LSPU, driven by progressive leadership, is a premier institution providing technology-mediated agriculture, fisheries and other related and emerging disciplines significantly contributing to the growth and development of the region and nation
Quality Policy
LSPU delivers quality education through responsive instruction, distinctive research, sustainable extension and production service. Thus, we are committed with continual improvement to meet applicable requirements to provide quality, efficient and effective services to the university stakeholder's highest level of satisfaction through an excellent management system imbued with utmost integrity, professionalism and innovation.
